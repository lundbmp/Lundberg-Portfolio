About Me

Thanks for finding my page.

My name is Michael, I'm a graduate of North Hennepin Community College with an assiociate in Computer Science. At North Hennepin I learned about: Object Oriented Programming, Data Structures, Algorithms, Embeded Programming(ARM specific), and Databases. While at North Hennepin I became proficient in Java, C#, Python, C++, and SQL.

I currently work at ECMC as an Operations Analyst. While at ECMC I've learned how to work in an Agile enviroment, R programming, managing and creating procedural documentation, as well as making complex SQL queries.

Work life balance is important and how I manage that is by having a variety of hobbies. Outside of work I like to: bake, archery, bike, run, play video games, and going to concerts.
